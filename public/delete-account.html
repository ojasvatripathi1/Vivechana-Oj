Account & Data Deletion

Vivechana Oj provides users with the ability to permanently delete their account and associated data directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Vivechana Oj app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion request

What Data Is Deleted

  • User account information
  • Authentication data
  • Saved preferences
  • User-generated data associated with the account

Data Retention Policy

All user data is permanently deleted immediately after account deletion and cannot be recovered.
